About & skills
A mix of structure and intuition. I like clean grids, brave type, and flows that feel like you’ve used them before.

Hi, I’m Varad — a UI/UX designer who builds product-ready interfaces inside Figma. I care a lot about visual clarity, copy that guides, and small interactions that make a product feel alive.

I enjoy taking messy ideas and turning them into structured screens, with clear hierarchy, smooth spacing, and components that are easy for devs to implement.

How I work
Simple, lightweight process that works well for freelance projects and quick experiments.
01 • Understand
Quick alignment

Short call or chat to understand your product, users, goals and constraints. We define the must-have screens and success metrics.

02 • Structure
Flows & wireframes

I map out key user flows and low-fidelity wireframes so we agree on structure before making it beautiful.

03 • Design
High-fidelity UI

We move into polished Figma designs: layout, type, colours, components and states. I share prototypes as we go.

04 • Handoff
Dev-ready files

Final Figma file with styles, components and comments for devs, plus exportable assets if needed.
